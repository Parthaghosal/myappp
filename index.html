Batman

The Caped Crusader

A tribute to The Dark Knight


  • 1982 - Batman is born at Gotham City Hospital under the name Bruce Wayne.
  • 1992 - Bruce Wayne witnesses his parents murder.
  • 1996 - At age 14, Bruce Wayne began his global sojourn, attending courses at Cambridge, the Sorbonne, and other European universities. However, he never stayed long and would often drop out after one semester.
  • 2002 - At age 20, Bruce Wayne attempted to join the FBI, but after learning about its regulations and conduct, Wayne deducted that he would never be able to completely oppose crime while working within the legal system.
  • 2007 - Bruce Wayne takes the name of Batman, and begins fighting crime running rampant in Gotham City.
  • 2010 - He met his greatest nemesis when the Joker first appeared, a brilliant serial killer dressing like a clown and murdering people uncontrollably. He had been exposed to chemicals in an accident that drove him completely insane, and he swore revenge on Gotham for creating him.
  • 2014 - The ultimate challenge is faced by Batman, he had to come to terms with what he is and saves Gotham City from complete destruction. Facing off with the deceptive enemy Ra's al Ghuls daughter and defeating her at a great cost to the city.
  • Present - Batman's whereabouts are unknown, but perhaps he will show up when he is needed most.
